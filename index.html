Our Services
Pick Up & Delivery

Our friendly and courteous staff will pick up and drop off your equipment during times that are convenient for you. We'll inspect your equipment throughout the entire time we are in possession of it to ensure complete quality and satisfaction.

Bulk & Custom Repairs

Whether you are a large school looking to repair the bulk of your seasonal athletic equipment or customer looking to repair trusted equipment, our facilities and trained staff can handle the load or unique request.

Seasonal Planning

Seasonal planning is critical to stay in the game and within budget. Our team can help you plan one team's equipment repairs or your entire school's. Successful planning saves time, money, and ensures your equipment is ready for the big game.

Trusted Advice

Athletic Equipment Repair owners and staff have decades of experience supporting local and regional customers. Our clients range from amateur athletes, high schools and colleges, as well as some of Milwaukee's Pro sports teams.
